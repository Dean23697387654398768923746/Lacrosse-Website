What is Lacrosse?


Lacrosse is a popular canadian sport. The easiest way to think of the game play is a mix between the RUnning from soccer, the hitting of hockey, and the fast pace scoring of basketball.

It was first played as early as 1100 AD to settle disputes and as a social event. After being played amoung many north american indigenous tribes for centuries, the french came along and observed it for a while. In 1856, a french settler created a rulebook with stick sizes, amounts of players and offside rules. THis changed the game forever. From then on, lacrosse was played by canadians, north americans and people across the globe. It became so wide spread that it was part of the 1904 and 1908 summer olympics.
